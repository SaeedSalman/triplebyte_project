Our Most Frequent Questions

  • What type of engineers are in your talent pool?

    We work primarily with back-end, front-end, full-stack, DevOps, machine learning, mobile, and generalist engineers of all kinds, as well as data science, distributed systems, and data pipeline specialists.

  • Why should my company use Triplebyte? What companies successfully use Triplebyte?

    Our partner companies range from seed-stage startups looking for their first engineer to big tech companies like Apple, Dropbox, and Snap, and their hiring needs are just as wide-ranging.

  • How do you assess engineers?

    Engineers complete various adaptive online quizzes (most quizzes are multiple-choice and some require actually writing code). Trained on over 300,000 engineers.
